# Creative Brief

## Film Title

Acme Skills Platform: Readiness With Proof

## Audience

Product and business leaders evaluating how the platform turns capability gaps into action.

## Business Question

Can a leader see a capability gap and understand the next action?

## Viewer Takeaway

After watching, the viewer should believe:

1. The product identifies a meaningful readiness gap.
2. The recommendation is grounded in evidence.
3. The output becomes a visible plan and record.

## Signature Format

Cinematic scroll story (`scroll-story`).

Recommendation reasoning: the audience must follow one capability gap through evidence, recommendation, and recorded outcome; continuity is the proof.

Alternate considered and why it lost: an editorial launch film would add announcement energy but compress the connected decision path.

## Evidence Treatment

Real UI proof with code-native framework graphics.

## Anchor Object

Customer discovery capability gap.

## Proof Points

| Claim | On-screen proof | Source screen |
|---|---|---|
| A readiness gap exists | Active gap card | Leader dashboard |
| The recommendation is explainable | Evidence rail | Plan screen |
| The outcome is recorded | Proof pills | Closing frame |
