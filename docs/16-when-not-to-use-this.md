# When Not To Use This

This workflow is powerful, but it is not always the right tool.

Use a simpler workflow when:

- You only need a quick internal screen recording.
- The product is changing daily and timing will not survive.
- There is no stable demo data.
- The story is mostly emotional and does not need product proof.
- The team cannot review frames or maintain a small render harness.

Use a different tool when:

- You need heavy cinematic editing with live footage.
- You need 3D character animation.
- You need real-time interactive demos instead of rendered films.
- You need a full video editor timeline.

This playbook is best when the product needs to prove something clearly and repeatedly.
