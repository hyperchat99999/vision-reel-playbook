# Showcase Music v2 Suite

The suite source contains four original sections generated with ElevenLabs Music v2 on 2026-07-19. The public scroll, collage, and hand-drawn masters use their matching sections. The editorial launch film deliberately retains its earlier tailored score, which fits that cut better.

- Seed: `84721`
- Section 1, `0–18s`: cinematic scroll momentum
- Section 2, `18–30s`: editorial launch energy
- Section 3, `30–45s`: tactile collage groove

The brief requested a 128 BPM feel, tight punchy drums, clean sub/bass separation, syncopated percussion, bright plucked synth and mallet motifs, short chapter impacts, minimal reverb, no vocals, no slow ambient intro, and a decisive final hit. `starter/scripts/apply-music-suite.cjs` slices, loudness-normalizes, and applies only the selected scroll, collage, and hand-drawn sections. The shipped project renderer remains paid-service-free and uses the improved deterministic score generator by default.
