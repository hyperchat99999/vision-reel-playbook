## Visual QA

- One tactile editorial system: uncoated paper grain, halftone hands, cream keylines, crisp cut edges, and layered shadows.
- Distinct compositions and background colors prevent the three beats from feeling like duplicates.
- Each frame uses a small number of large semantic groups with generous negative space.
- No generated typography, logos, watermarks, subtitles, or fake readable UI text.
- Motion prompts must preserve these objects and relationships, favor connected paper mechanics, and avoid camera shake or random object drift.
