# Shot List

Use this for hybrid films that include generated human clips.

| Shot | Type | Duration | Purpose | Inputs | Approval Gate |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|---|
| S01 | Character setup | 6s | Human context | Contact sheet, scene prompt | First/last frame approved |
| S02 | UI proof | 12s | Product demonstrates claim | Real app state, voiceover | Contact sheet frame grabs approved |
| S03 | Closing | 8s | Business outcome | Framework scene, voiceover | Final frame approved |

## Shot Types

- Character setup: generated or filmed human context.
- UI proof: real product capture.
- Bridge: transition from human context into product UI.
- Framework: simplified logic animation.
- Closing: outcome summary.
